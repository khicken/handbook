The internet handbook

Advice and dangers told by the first generation raised with the internet.

why it was made

Gen Z is the world's first generation of "digital natives", in which digital technology and the internet have been an integral part of their everyday life. From education to social health, the role of the internet on younger individuals brings many unspoken consequences.

The Internet Handbook is a simple, open-access book detailing these consequences, as well as how to fix the negatives.
